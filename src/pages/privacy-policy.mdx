# Brantial Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: September 09, 2025_

This Privacy Policy explains how Brantial AI ("Brantial" or "we"), developed and operated by Webtures LTD., collects, processes, and protects your personal data when you use our website and services ("Services"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy.

---

## 1. Data Controller

Under GDPR, the data controller is:

WEBTURES LTD.  
494a Fulham Road, London, England, SW6 5NH  
Email: [support@brantial.ai](mailto:support@brantial.ai)

## 2. Data We Collect

When you use our Services, we may collect the following categories of personal and technical data:

- IP address, browser type, access time
- Referrer URL and device metadata
- Prompts submitted by you
- AI-generated outputs based on your prompts
- (If authorized) Google Search Console and Google Analytics data
- (For subscriptions) Stripe payment information and billing data

---

## 3. Purposes of Processing

- To provide AI-generated outputs tailored to your prompts
- To generate analytics and reports about your website visibility and performance
- To manage subscription and payment processes
- To improve service functionality, security, and user experience
- To comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing

- GDPR Article 6(1)(a): Consent (e.g., analytics cookies, marketing permissions)
- GDPR Article 6(1)(b): Contract necessity (provision of membership and subscription services)
- GDPR Article 6(1)(c): Legal obligation (billing and tax compliance requirements)
- GDPR Article 6(1)(f): Legitimate interests (system security, performance monitoring, fraud prevention)

---

## 5. Third-Party Service Providers

- AI service providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI, etc.) – for processing prompts and generating AI outputs
- Google Search Console & Google Analytics – for visibility and engagement analytics
- Stripe – for subscription and payment processing
- Cloudflare – for performance, DNS, and security

These providers may process your IP address, technical identifiers, and, when necessary, your prompt content under their own privacy policies.

---

## 6. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers are located outside the European Economic Area (EEA), such as in the United States. We ensure that such transfers are based on the European Commission’s approved Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

---

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use essential cookies for core functionality and Google Analytics cookies for usage analytics. You may control your cookie preferences via your browser settings. Non-essential cookies are only enabled with your consent.

---

## 8. Data Retention

We store your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined above or to comply with legal retention requirements.

---

## 9. Your Rights

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

- Request access to your data
- Request rectification of inaccurate data
- Request erasure of your data
- Object to or restrict processing
- Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (data portability)

To exercise your rights, please contact us at [support@brantial.ai](mailto:support@brantial.ai).

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. The current version will always be published on our website.
