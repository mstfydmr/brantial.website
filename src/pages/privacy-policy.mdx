# Privacy Policy

_Last Updated: August 27, 2025_

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our services, including our website, platform, and related tools. Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with its terms, please discontinue use of our services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

### 1.1 Personal Information

- Name, email address, phone number
- Billing and payment details
- Account login credentials

### 1.2 Non-Personal Information

- Browser type, device type, operating system
- IP address and geolocation data
- Usage statistics, logs, cookies

### 1.3 Sensitive Information (if applicable)

- Only when voluntarily provided and necessary for the service
- Always stored and processed in compliance with GDPR/CCPA

---

## 2. How We Collect Information

We may collect information through:

- **Direct interactions:** Registration forms, subscriptions, customer support
- **Automated technologies:** Cookies, analytics, tracking pixels
- **Third parties:** Business partners, service providers, payment processors

---

## 3. How We Use Information

Your information may be used for:

- Providing, operating, and improving our services
- Processing payments and billing
- Personalizing user experience
- Sending updates, newsletters, and marketing communications (with consent)
- Ensuring security, fraud detection, and compliance with legal obligations
- Analyzing usage trends and performance

---

## 4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Compliance)

We process personal data under these legal grounds:

- Consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR)
- Contractual necessity (Article 6(1)(b) GDPR)
- Legal obligation (Article 6(1)(c) GDPR)
- Legitimate interests (Article 6(1)(f) GDPR)

---

## 5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do **not** sell or rent personal data.  
We may share data only with:

- **Service providers:** Hosting, analytics, payment processing
- **Business partners:** When necessary to deliver services
- **Legal authorities:** When required by law or to protect rights
- **Mergers & acquisitions:** In case of business transfer

---

## 6. Data Retention

- We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined.
- Account-related data is retained until deletion request.
- Legal and financial records may be kept longer in compliance with law.

---

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

- We use cookies to improve user experience, analyze trends, and personalize content.
- You can control or disable cookies in your browser settings.
- Third-party cookies (e.g., Google Analytics) may also be used.

---

## 8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard safeguards:

- Encryption (SSL/TLS, data-at-rest encryption)
- Firewalls and intrusion detection
- Access controls and authentication protocols
- Regular audits and vulnerability testing

---

## 9. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
- Request deletion (“Right to be Forgotten”)
- Restrict or object to processing
- Data portability
- Opt-out of marketing communications
- For California residents: opt-out of sale/sharing of personal information

To exercise rights, contact us at: **[privacy@brantial.ai]**
