# Privacy Policy

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.<br />
Scalar does not collect any personal data unless explicitly provided by you. If you use our cloud features, we may store necessary account information like your email address for authentication and service-related communication.<br />
We do not share your data with third parties, and all data is handled securely. For our open-source version, no data is collected or transmitted.<br />
For any questions or concerns about your data, feel free to [contact us](mailto:support@scalar.com).

## GDPR Compliance

If you're located in the EU, you have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To make a request, simply [contact us](mailto:support@scalar.com). We process data in accordance with the GDPR and only retain it as long as necessary for the services you use.

## Cookies

Our site uses essential cookies to enable basic functionality like login and preferences. We may also use cookies to understand how visitors use the site to improve usability.

## Open Source

Using the open-source Scalar CMS? We don't collect any data at all. Everything runs locally or in your own environment.
