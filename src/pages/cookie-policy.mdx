# Brantial Cookie Policy

_Last Updated: September 09, 2025_

This Cookie Policy ("Policy") explains the use of cookies and similar technologies on the Brantial AI platform ("Brantial"), developed and operated by Webtures LTD. ("Webtures", "we"). This Policy forms an integral part of the Brantial Privacy Policy. By using Brantial, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Policy.

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They allow websites to recognize your device, remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and improve your experience.

Other technologies such as pixel tags, SDKs, and local storage may also be used; collectively referred to as "cookies" in this Policy.

---

## 2. Types of Cookies

Cookies can be classified as follows:

**a) By Duration:**

- Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser.
- Persistent Cookies: Stored on your device for a set period.

**b) By Source:**

- First-Party Cookies: Set directly by Brantial.
- Third-Party Cookies: Set by providers such as Google, Stripe, Cloudflare, etc.

**c) By Purpose:**

- Strictly Necessary Cookies: Required for core functions (login, security, payments).
- Analytics and Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the site (e.g., Google Analytics).
- Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences (language, theme, settings).
- Marketing and Advertising Cookies: Deliver personalized content or ads (enabled only with consent).

## 5. Managing Cookies

You can block or delete cookies from your browser settings. Analytics, functional, and marketing cookies are only enabled with your consent. You can update your preferences anytime via the Cookie Management Tool on the Brantial website.

Note: Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent Brantial from functioning properly.
